What does vlr.gg do and why I like the website

Andrew Egan
10/5/25
CPCS 20000

The website is used to track the many different leaugues in the professional Valorant scene.
The website makes it very easy to follow teams throughout the season and it also have news on each team so it easy to keep track of when a player is dropped by and team.
When looking at specific matches there are little forum underneath them that people in the community can talk about the match even before it starts.
It also makes it very easy to track the brackets in tournaments and how they are progressing.
